FAQs

Can I request a product review?

Absolutely! We're always interested in hearing about products our readers would like us to review. Use the contact form above or email us directly with your suggestion.

Do you accept review units from manufacturers?

We may accept review units for testing purposes, provided we can review them honestly and independently. Any products provided by manufacturers are clearly disclosed in the review.

Can I advertise on AffiliateReviews UK?

How can I stay updated on new reviews?

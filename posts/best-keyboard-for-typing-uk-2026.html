Buying Guide

  • Noise level: If you share a room, go quieter. If you live alone, click away without guilt.
  • Key feel: Mechanical usually feels better for heavy typing.
  • Layout size: Compact saves desk space; full size gives you a number pad.
  • Wrist comfort: Consider a wrist rest if you type for hours.
  • Wireless vs wired: Wireless is cleaner; wired is simpler and always ready.

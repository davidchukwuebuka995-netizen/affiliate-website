Buying Guide

When choosing a kettle, consider these key factors:

  • Capacity: Most kettles hold 1.5-1.7 litres, enough for 6-7 cups
  • Speed: Look for rapid boil features if you're in a hurry
  • Material: Stainless steel is durable, glass looks stylish, plastic is affordable
  • Features: Temperature control, keep-warm function, quiet boil
  • Safety: Auto shut-off and boil-dry protection are essential

What to Look for in Budget Earbuds

Sound Quality

Under ¬£50, you won't get studio-quality sound, but you should expect balanced, clear audio suitable for music and calls. Look for earbuds with at least 5.8mm drivers.

Battery Life

Minimum 5 hours per charge is acceptable; 8+ hours is excellent. Don't forget the charging case‚Äîit typically provides 24-32 additional hours.

Noise Cancellation

Budget ANC is passable for trains and offices but won't match premium models. If you need strong ANC, expect to spend more.

Comfort & Fit

You'll wear these for hours. Look for multiple ear tip sizes and lightweight designs. Check reviews mentioning comfort for extended wear.

Water Resistance

IPX4 or better protects against sweat and light rain. Essential if you'll use them during exercise.

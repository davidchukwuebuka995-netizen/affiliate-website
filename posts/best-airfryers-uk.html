Air Fryer Buying Guide

Size & Capacity

Air fryer capacity is measured in litres. A 4L capacity is suitable for individuals and couples, while 5.5L+ works better for families of 4 or more. Dual basket models give you flexibility without taking up too much space.

Power & Heat

Higher wattage (1500W+) heats faster and maintains consistent temperatures. Look for models with temperatures ranging from 40°C to 200°C for maximum versatility.

Controls

Choose between mechanical dials (simple, fewer things to break) or digital touchscreen (more presets, easier temperature control). Both work well—it's personal preference.

Design & Aesthetics

Air fryers are counter-top appliances, so think about where it will sit. Modern designs from brands like Cosori and Philips look sleek and professional.

Common Air Fryer Questions

How much oil do I use?

Little to none! Most recipes use just 1-2 teaspoons of oil (or cooking spray) to achieve that crispy texture. This is dramatically less than traditional frying.

How long do air fryers last?

Quality air fryers typically last 5-10 years with regular use. Premium brands like Philips often come with 2-year warranties.

Can I use frozen food?

Yes! Air fryers work brilliantly with frozen food. Just add 5-10 minutes to cooking time and adjust the temperature slightly.

Is it worth the money?

If you cook regularly, absolutely. Air fryers use less electricity than ovens, reduce oil consumption, and cook faster—saving money over time.
